# Lead
Tens of thousands of young Nepalese protesters have taken to the streets demanding constitutional reforms and greater political accountability.

# The Movement
- Scale: 77+ killed in protests
- Leaders: Youth activists
- Demands: Constitutional reform, accountability
- Focus: Balen Shah vs. KP Sharma Oli

# What's at Stake
- Constitution: Written in 2015, seen as flawed
- Power: Youth want more representation
- Economy: Jobs, opportunity, future

# The Kicker
Nepal's youth are demanding a seat at the table. The old guard can either share power or watch the country transform without them.

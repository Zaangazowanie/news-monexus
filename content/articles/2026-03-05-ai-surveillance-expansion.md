# Lead
AI-powered surveillance systems are expanding rapidly across the globe, with governments and corporations deploying sophisticated facial recognition and behavior prediction technologies.

# The Expansion
- Facial recognition: Now in 75+ countries
- Behavior prediction: AI analyzing patterns
- Integration: Cameras, phones, social media
- Spending: $50 billion annually

# What's Being Hidden
- The accuracy disparities by ethnicity/gender
- How data is shared between governments
- The lack of meaningful consent

# The Kicker
We're building a world where every step is tracked, every face is scanned, and privacy is becoming a memory.

# Lead
Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney said he wants de-escalation of the Iran war but declined to rule out military participation, signaling that Canada may be drawn into the expanding Middle East conflict.

# What Carney Said
- Position: Wants "de-escalation" of Iran attacks
- Refused to rule out: Military involvement
- Context: Growing pressure from NATO allies

# What's Being Hidden
- Canada has existing military commitments in the Middle East
- NATO solidarity pressures are intense
- No parliamentary vote on war powers

# The Kicker
The PM says he wants peace but won't rule out war. Canada's next move could define its role in the Middle East for decades.

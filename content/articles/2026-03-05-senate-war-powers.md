# Lead
The Senate voted 47-53 against a measure that would have required congressional approval for military action against Iran, effectively granting President Trump unchecked authority to wage war without legislative oversight.

# The Vote
- Final tally: 47-53
- Needed: 60 votes to pass

# Key Votes
Voted FOR restrictions: Rand Paul (R-KY), Bernie Sanders (I-VT), John Fetterman (D-PA)
Voted AGAINST: Majority of Republicans

# What's Being Hidden
- Congress is actively abdicating its constitutional war powers
- The 1973 War Powers Resolution is being ignored
- No debate on actual Iran strategy
- Troops are already deployed with no congressional approval

# The Kicker
Congress just voted to let the President wage war on Iran without them. The constitutional balance of power has shifted dramatically.

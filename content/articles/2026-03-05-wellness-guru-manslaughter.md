# Lead

A NSW wellness guru has been charged with manslaughter more than four years after his spiritual retreat client died from a perforated oesophagus after consuming ayahuasca and kambo (frog toxin) during a ceremony at the Arcoora retreat near Byron Bay.

# The Case

- **Accused:** Soulore Solaris, 52 (formerly Peter George McIntyre)
- **Victim:** Jarrad Antonovich, 47
- **Date:** October 16, 2021
- **Location:** Arcoora retreat, Collins Creek, ~60km from Byron Bay
- **Cause of death:** Perforated oesophagus after consuming ayahuasca + kambo
- **Charge:** Manslaughter (arrested Tuesday, March 3, 2026)

# What Happened

- Solaris presided over ceremony using ayahuasca + kambo
- Allegedly provided ayahuasca to Antonovich
- Accused of delaying medical treatment after Antonovich collapsed
- Kambo = toxin scraped from giant Amazon tree frog (Schedule 10 poison, banned in Australia)
- Ayahuasca = plant-based psychedelic (banned 2022)
